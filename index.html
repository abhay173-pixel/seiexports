About Savitri Engineering Industries

Company Legacy: Established in 1985, Savitri Engineering Industries has built a strong global reputation in the manufacturing and export of diesel engines, generators, pump sets, corn grinders, discs, sharpeners, and spare parts.

Global Reach & Commitment: With a focus on performance, durability, and customer satisfaction, we have proudly served clients across Latin America, Africa, Middle East, Australia, and Europe—delivering reliable and cost-effective solutions.

Technology & Applications: Our products are engineered using advanced technology and thoroughly tested to meet international standards—suitable for agricultural, industrial, and commercial use.

About Agra: Located in Agra, a city known for the Taj Mahal and rich craftsmanship, our roots are built on traditional values and strong industrial ethics, supporting a wide industrial base in North India.
